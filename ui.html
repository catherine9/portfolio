The Process

Savoir Fare is a small establishment located at Algonquin College. This was a group project to help identify what the chefs need in order to make the process better and more efficient. (The bottom left drawing in designed by Glenis El-Beyrouti and bottom right is designed by Sara Habel)

The Result

My group and I were able to create a high fidelity wireframe, that executes what the app is capable of. This gives an advantage for chefs when they are cooking new recipes for the eatery and customers an opporunity to view the recipes better.

Video Example

The design layout for the app is broken down into three tasks. The main task in the video is creating a new recipe. This will better explain what the app is capable of doing to meet the users needs.
