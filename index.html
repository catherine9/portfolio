Who am I?

Hello my name is Catherine Legault. I am a graphic designer, motion designer, and illustrator. I graduated Algonquin College in 2019 and worked for different marketing agencies such as Cyan Solutions, Algonquin College, Invest Ottawa, and Victory Arts where I focus my passion on 2D animations and motion graphics. I also do freelance motion design and graphic design. I like to tell stories through each of my videos and try to expand my creative skills for each video. I enjoy watching Anime, Marvel films, Disney, playing video games as well as collecting geeky stuff. On my spare time I enjoy knife throwing, drawing on my ipad, and building up my small etsy shop where I sell prints, stickers and pins.
