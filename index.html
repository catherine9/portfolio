Catherine Legault

Graphic Designer

Hello my name is Catherine Legault. I am a graphic designer, motion designer, and illustrator. I like to try new skills and expand my creativity in many of my projects. I enjoy creating things inside and outside my worklife. On my spare time I enjoy watching anime, playing video games as well as making cute illustrated prints and stickers. When I am not doing anything design realted, I love being out in the country with my boyfriend throwing knives and axes, shooting guns or just simply chilling with my pets.
