Summary

I took a co-op opportunity to work with Tero in Victory Arts and assisted in creating videos for thier shows called "Me and my Monster" and YOUniverse. These primary shows I worked on are directed towards a very young demographic that includes lessons and facts. I was able to expand my creativity on a design level where I work on different mediums to tell a story.

Video Results

The bone motif was the official logo used in multiple final products such as the calander, pop-up banners, postcards as well as the small promo video.
