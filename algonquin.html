The Process

While working at Algonquin College I was asked to create a 2019 calendar. As part of the Paws 4 Stress campaign that is advertised across campus. The therapy dogs get a chance to have an illustrated counterpart for the calendar. To stick with the Algonquin college graphic standards each therapy dog had to have the geometric shapes with no curves or gradients.

The Result

The calendar will help promote the therapy dogs that come to the campus to destress college students. The bone motif is our primary feature in the calendar as it displayed between each month and a different color to represent the holiday color while maintaining the Algonquin Graphic Standards.
