Position:

Graphic Designer

Branding | Illustration | Motion Design

I worked as a Junior Graphic Designer for Algonquin College as a student job in their marketing department. The position was only open to students at the college, meaning it ended when I started the co-op placement in my program. I created and updated illustrated avatars for staff members on the Algonquin College staff board. I also drafted and edited video ads, creating short GIFs for the Algonquin College website and social media. I helped create the entire branding scheme for the Algonquin Dog Squad campaign. (Charity Event) I created an illustrated avatar for each dog, created a calendar with each dog in the picture, designed the pop-up banner, post card, as well as create the animated ad for the Dog Squad using the avatars to showcase around the college. The campaign was popular to make it to a CTV news article. Here are examples of my work below.
