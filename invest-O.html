Summary

I got the opportunity to work as a motion designer under an 11 month remote contract with Invest Ottawa during the pandemic. The original position was for a graphic design proof where I helped out with standard graphic design for brands. I got the opportunity to be transferred to another department within Invest Ottawa to be their motion designer for social media and comapnies such as AreaX.O and Bayview Yards.

Bayview Yards Video Concepts

My largest project opportunity was to work with Bayview Yards to create a journey video. The journey would be based on a robot going through different stages from a rough sketch, all the way to its final form which is a 3D robot built in Blender for the first time. The robot went through multiple sketches, storyboarding, rough, and clean drafts before it was sent off to After Effects to go through the basic rigging and then stitch it together in the larger composition where it is the base of the bayview yards project.
