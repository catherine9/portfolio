Invest Ottawa

Design | Motion

Summary

I got the opportunity to work as a motion designer under an 11 month remote contract with Invest Ottawa during the pandemic. The original position was for a graphic design proof where I helped out with standard graphic design for brands. I got the opportunity to be transferred to another department within Invest Ottawa to be their motion designer for social media and comapnies such as AreaX.O and Bayview Yards.

Concepts

While the layout designs were done by the graphic designers. I worked on the aniamtion part of each video. There were times I had to create my own layouts for more simple animations that will be posted on social media for only 5-10 seconds. I worked on multiple animations, the main one being the Digital Main Street or DMS concepts

Final Result

Some of the final results in these concepts have a minimal feel to each of them that still has the arranged layout given to me, or my own designs that best represents the company I am working with. With each video I help create gets reviewed by my co-workers for feedback and then published to social media. Each video usually goes through multiple iterations or storyboarding so they meet the ideal final result.
